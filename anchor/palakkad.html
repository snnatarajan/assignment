PARAMBIKULAM WILD LIFE SANCTUARY

Parambikulam Wild Life Sanctuary is a Tiger reserve also. It spreads over the lavishing area of over 285 sq. Km. One thing you should never miss here is the Kannimara teak tree, which is believed to be more than 350 years old.

SIRUVANI DAM

Built-in a classical Kerala and Tamil Nadu architectural style, this dam is a successful project of the Kerala government to meet the drinking water demand of the adjacent townships. The dam stands in the Siruvani River and is said to be built in 1927.
