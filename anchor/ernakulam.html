FORT KOCHI

Fort Kochi is not about historical fortifications, canons and ruins of old palatial buildings. Instead, it houses the vibe of an ancient colonial city with its cobbled streets, colourful and charming buildings and a picturesque view of the beach, and is one of the best places to see in Ernakulam

WILLINGDON ISLAND

Beautifully housed in the heart of Kochi, the 82-year old Willingdon Island showcases the spectacular prowess and the intellectual level of human mind, and is counted as one of the best places to see in Ernakulam.

KADAMBRAYAR

Kadambrayar, which is situated at Kizhakambalam grama panchayat in Ernakulam district, is a sleepy hamlet oozing with the charm of a quintessential Kerala village and is an ideal destination for a one-day hangout
