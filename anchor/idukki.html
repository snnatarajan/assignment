KALVARY MOUNT

Put on your hiking boots to climb up the Kalvari Mount/Calvary Mount, a vantage point that is also a sacred site for the Christian. The site's surroundings feature beautiful homestays of Idukki.

WILD LIFE SANCTUARY

Steep mountains, valleys, hills are sprawled across the 77 square kilometers of the area at an elevation of 450 m to 748 m (approx.) above sea level. Hence, Idukki Wildlife Sanctuary is a crowd-pulling tourist attraction

RAINBOW FALLS

The name itself suggests that a visit to this waterfall is all about seeing a moonbow and lunar rainbow. Here water gushes from the height of 1,500 m (approx.) and makes hues of the rainbow, which is a delight for shutterbugs.
